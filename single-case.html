CASE DETAILS

Project Background

Henan Recycle Machinery Co Ltd supplied a fully automated solar panel recycling system to our UK client in May 2025. This cutting-edge facility represents a significant advancement in renewable energy waste management, addressing the growing challenge of end-of-life solar panel disposal.

The client, a leading recycling company in the United Kingdom, sought a comprehensive solution capable of processing various types of photovoltaic panels while maximizing material recovery rates. After extensive evaluation of available technologies, they selected our advanced recycling line for its superior separation efficiency and environmental benefits.

Technical Solution

Our solar panel recycling machine employs a multi-stage processing approach that carefully separates different components of waste solar panels. The system begins with automated feeding and pre-treatment, followed by precision cutting and material separation processes.

The technology utilizes advanced mechanical and thermal separation techniques to recover valuable materials including aluminum frames, tempered glass, silicon wafers, plastic junction boxes, and metal conductors. Each component is processed through specialized equipment designed to maintain material purity and maximize recovery rates.

Key Features

  • High Processing Capacity: Capable of handling 500-1000 kg of waste solar panels per hour
  • Advanced Separation Technology: Achieves over 95% material recovery rate
  • Automated Operation: Minimal manual intervention required, reducing labor costs
  • Environmental Compliance: Equipped with dust collection and emission control systems
  • Quality Output: Produces high-purity recycled materials ready for reuse

Installation & Commissioning

Our experienced engineering team completed the installation and commissioning of the recycling line within a 6-week timeframe. The process included comprehensive site preparation, equipment installation, system integration, and thorough testing to ensure optimal performance.

During the commissioning phase, we provided extensive training to the client's operational staff, covering equipment operation, maintenance procedures, safety protocols, and troubleshooting techniques. This hands-on training program ensured a smooth transition to full production capacity.

Project Results

Since commencing operations, the facility has successfully processed thousands of waste solar panels, recovering valuable materials for reintegration into manufacturing supply chains. The client reports high satisfaction with the system's reliability, efficiency, and ease of operation.

The recovered materials, including aluminum, glass, and silicon, have found ready markets, contributing to the circular economy and reducing the environmental impact of solar panel waste. This project demonstrates the viability and importance of solar panel recycling as the renewable energy sector continues to expand.

Client Testimonial

"The solar panel recycling system from Henan Recycle Machinery has exceeded our expectations in every aspect. The technology is robust, efficient, and delivers consistent results. Their professional support throughout the project, from initial consultation to after-sales service, has been exemplary. We are proud to contribute to sustainable waste management with this advanced equipment."

Conclusion

This project exemplifies our commitment to providing innovative recycling solutions that address emerging environmental challenges. As the solar energy industry grows, efficient recycling systems become increasingly critical for managing end-of-life panels and recovering valuable materials.

We continue to support our UK client with ongoing technical assistance and are pleased to see the positive environmental and economic impact of this installation. This success story reinforces our position as a leading provider of advanced recycling technology.
